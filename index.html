Welcome to Up North Travel

Located on the top half of North America, Canada is the second largest country in the world with a land mass of over 9.9 million km2. The diversity and beautiful scenery is even larger! This country is surrounded by the 3 out of the 5 worlds oceans. The Atlantic Ocean largely on the East, The Pacific Ocean to the West and the Arctic Ocean on the Territories of Canada. We have the largest coastline in the world and also the longest land border along the 49th parallel. With a population of over 38 million people, most live near the southern border which neighbors the United States.

What makes Canada is the vast diversity. You can find a little bit of all over the world in Canada. The Canadian culture is made up of every other culture. There are only 2 official languages which are English and French. Canadians are more than happy to help if you speak neither of the 2 languages. We are one of the world's friendliest people and communicating with us is a positive experience. There is so much to learn about Canadians and other cultures that make Canada a great place to be all year round. Mostly in the summer-time if you don't prefer the snow. The snow experience may be cold but it will be well worth it that you got to experience it.
